About Us

OIT Computers commenced operation in 2008 - Designing, Implementing and Maintaining Computer Networks. Marketing computer hardware and software - offering training, website design and allied consultancy services. Over the years, the company has evolved into an IT consulting firm providing flexible, easy-to-use and secure database management systems application software for a variety of clients operations on stand alone and networked computers as well as on an intranet and the internet for some of them.
